Visit us

We'd love to meet you at one of our meetings! You can attend in person or online. Just sign up on Eventbrite, or drop us an email (hac.toastmasters@gmail.com) and we'll add you to the guest list.

If you are attending:

  • in person: note that photo ID will be required to enter the venue and there is a dress code.
  • virtually: we'll send you the details to join the online video call (using Zoom).

Venue

Our venue is quite unique amongst Toastmasters clubs. We're proudly based in Amoury House, the grand home (and active barracks) of the Honorable Artillery Company (HAC), the oldest regiment of the British Army. You'll find pictures of the venue across this website.

Finding the venue

The HAC
Armoury House, City Road
London
EC1Y 2BQ

View in Apple Maps.

We are a short walk from the Moorgate, Old Street and Liverpool Street Stations.

The entrance is along City Road, go through the gates (that look like they belong on a castle) that are opposite the Tesco Express. There you will find the guardhouse, greet them, explain that you're here for the Toastmasters club and show them your photo ID.

If you have any problems, feel free to contact us (we'll share our contact details when you sign up).

Dress code

Due to the traditions of our venue there is a dress code, gents are expected to wear a jacket and collared shirt (tie optional) and for ladies, formal work attire is sufficient.
